About Us

A Student Club like no other

ATPIS is an initiative made by High-Schoolers of SSCC Kfarhbab, Lebanon, whose mission is to support projects initiated by one or more club members in scientific, technological and technical subjects, operating on a voluntary basis.

Read More

Student-led

ATPIS is led by students under the guidance of a mentor, encouraging entrepreneurial and managerial skills.

Science & Technology

The club is oriented towards all forms of Science & Technology, including Chemistry, Math, Physics, Biology, Robotics, IT...

Collaborative Environment

Members work in team on innovative projects, fostering mutual support, and leading to growth and success.

Return to the Community

Our objective is also to improve scientific interest in our school, which was the main factor behind creating the club!

Specialized Groups

The club is structured into 4 distinct groups, each specialized in key scientific areas.

Chemistry

Discover the fascinating world of chemistry through exciting hands-on experiments. From creating colorful reactions to exploring the properties of different materials, this group brings the concepts of atoms, molecules, and compounds to life in a fun and engaging way.

Math & Physics

Explore the forces that shape our world through hands-on physics projects! From building circuits to studying motion, energy, and waves, this group dives deep into the practical side of physics while using math as a powerful tool to explain and predict how things work.

Biology & Ecology

Get up close with the science of living things and their environments. This group focuses on hands-on activities like examining ecosystems, studying biodiversity, and developing sustainable solutions to environmental challenges—all while understanding the critical connections between life and nature.

ACM

Dive into the exciting world of technology and coding. Whether it’s programming apps, solving puzzles, or building interactive projects, this group empowers you to turn creative ideas into reality while learning the fundamentals of computer science.
